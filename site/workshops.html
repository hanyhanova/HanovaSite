Executive Education

Workshops for leaders
who act on insight.

Hanova executive workshops are intensive, senior-led programs designed to give leadership teams the frameworks, clarity, and decision-making tools to lead transformation with confidence.

Our Approach

Not training. Executive enablement.

Every Hanova workshop is designed around a specific leadership challenge — not generic content. Participants leave with actionable frameworks, organizational diagnostics, and implementation roadmaps they can deploy immediately within their own context.

All workshops are facilitated by senior Hanova principals with direct operational experience in the subject area. No slides without substance. No theory without practice.

Core Programs

Our Workshop Portfolio

Healthcare Leadership

Healthcare Transformation for Executives

A strategic overview of healthcare system transformation — covering organizational redesign, quality governance (JCI/CBAHI), patient journey optimization, and Vision 2030 health sector alignment. Designed for hospital CEOs, CMOs, COOs, and board members.

Outcomes: Transformation readiness assessment · Priority mandate identification · 90-day action plan

Data Strategy

Data-Driven Decision Making

Built for senior leaders who need to understand — and lead — the shift from intuition-based to evidence-based decision-making. Covers data governance essentials, BI literacy for executives, and how to commission and interpret analytical outputs without technical expertise.

Outcomes: Executive BI framework · Data literacy roadmap · Governance design principles

Performance Management

KPI Governance & Performance Management

A deep dive into designing, deploying, and governing organizational KPI systems. Covers KPI architecture, cascading performance frameworks, accountability design, and the behavioral dynamics of performance culture. Applicable across healthcare, government, and enterprise.

Outcomes: KPI architecture blueprint · Governance charter · Dashboard design brief

Financial Performance

Revenue & Operations Optimization

A practical executive program covering revenue cycle management, operational efficiency levers, and financial performance governance. Participants gain frameworks for identifying and prioritizing high-impact improvement opportunities within their own organizational context.

Outcomes: Revenue opportunity map · Operational diagnostic · Prioritized initiative roadmap

Experience Design

Patient / Customer Journey Optimization

Journey mapping as a strategic tool — not just a service design exercise. This workshop equips executive teams to understand, measure, and systematically improve end-to-end patient or customer experiences, linking experience improvements to financial and operational outcomes.

Outcomes: Current-state journey map · Pain point prioritization · CX governance framework

Digital Leadership

Digital Transformation for Leaders

A non-technical but rigorous executive program on leading digital transformation. Covers technology investment governance, digital roadmap development, vendor management, change leadership, and the organizational capabilities required to sustain digital advantage in a competitive landscape.

Outcomes: Digital maturity assessment · 3-year roadmap framework · Investment governance model
