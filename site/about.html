Who We Are

An advisory built on execution, not just analysis.

Hanova Consultancy is a premium management consulting firm headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. We work with C-suite executives and organizational leaders across healthcare, government, financial services, and enterprise sectors to design and execute transformation programs that deliver measurable, lasting results.

Our approach is distinguished by physician-led strategic thinking, deep multi-entity operational experience, and an unwavering commitment to KPI-governed outcomes. We do not deliver binders. We deliver change.

Every engagement is structured around a clear performance mandate — with defined metrics, governance frameworks, and execution timelines that keep leadership informed and transformation on track from day one.
