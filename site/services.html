Practice Areas

What We Do

Six integrated practice areas — each designed to address the full spectrum of organizational transformation, from diagnostic clarity to embedded, measurable results.

01

Strategy & Transformation

We design and execute organizational transformation programs that translate executive vision into operational reality. Our strategic engagements begin with rigorous diagnostic work — mapping current-state capabilities, performance gaps, and market positioning — before moving into program design and structured execution.

Unlike traditional strategy consultancies, Hanova does not exit after the roadmap is delivered. We remain embedded through implementation, governing outcomes against defined KPIs and adapting strategy to emerging realities.

Business Value

Provides leadership with a clear, executable strategic direction — aligned across functions, prioritized by impact, and governed by performance metrics that make accountability visible at every level.

Example Outcomes

  • Organizational redesign reducing management layers by 30%, improving decision velocity
  • Corporate strategy development aligned to Vision 2030 sector transformation goals
  • Transformation program governance framework enabling real-time board-level visibility
  • Post-merger integration strategy for multi-entity healthcare system
02

Operations & Revenue Optimization

Operational inefficiency is one of the most significant and least visible sources of organizational value destruction. Hanova conducts deep operational diagnostics — mapping workflows, identifying bottlenecks, quantifying waste, and redesigning processes for maximum throughput and financial performance.

Our revenue optimization work spans the full revenue cycle: pricing strategy, capacity utilization, billing compliance, denial management, and patient or customer journey monetization — all governed by KPI dashboards that make improvement measurable.

Business Value

Turns operational complexity into a competitive advantage — enabling organizations to do more with existing resources while simultaneously improving quality, compliance, and customer experience.

Example Outcomes

  • Revenue cycle redesign increasing net collections by 28% within 6 months
  • Workflow optimization reducing average patient wait times by 40%
  • Capacity utilization analysis unlocking 22% additional throughput without new headcount
  • Procurement rationalization delivering 18% cost reduction in a government entity
03

Data & Business Intelligence

Organizations cannot govern what they cannot see. Hanova builds end-to-end data infrastructure — from source system integration and data warehousing to executive dashboards, KPI governance frameworks, and predictive analytics — that gives leadership a single source of truth for every critical decision.

We design for sustainability: all BI architecture is built to be maintainable by internal teams, with governance documentation, training, and data quality protocols embedded from day one.

Business Value

Converts fragmented, siloed data into a strategic asset — enabling faster, more confident decisions at every organizational level and creating the analytical foundation for AI and automation investment.

Example Outcomes

  • Enterprise BI platform consolidating 12 source systems into a unified executive dashboard
  • KPI governance framework deployed across 6 hospitals, enabling system-wide performance benchmarking
  • Predictive demand model reducing pharmacy stockouts by 35%
  • Financial analytics infrastructure reducing monthly close cycle from 12 days to 4
04

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is not a technology project — it is an organizational change initiative enabled by technology. Hanova designs digital transformation programs that begin with business outcomes and work backwards to technology selection, implementation, and adoption — ensuring that every digital investment generates measurable organizational value.

We support leaders through the full digital journey: strategy development, vendor selection, program governance, change management, and capability building — maintaining accountability for outcomes throughout.

Business Value

Enables organizations to compete in a digital-first environment — with scalable systems, data-driven processes, and digital-ready leadership that can adapt to emerging technologies without dependency on external consultants.

Example Outcomes

  • 5-year digital roadmap for a 3,000-bed hospital network, fully aligned to clinical and financial KPIs
  • EHR implementation program governance reducing go-live delays by 60%
  • Digital channel strategy increasing self-service adoption by 45% in a financial institution
  • Change management framework achieving 87% system adoption within 90 days of go-live
05

Integration & Interoperability

Most large organizations operate a fragmented technology landscape — systems that do not communicate, data that cannot be trusted, and processes that require manual intervention to bridge the gaps. Hanova designs and implements integration architectures that connect systems, automate data flows, and create the interoperability foundation required for advanced analytics and automation.

We specialize in healthcare interoperability (HL7 FHIR, DICOM), financial data integration, and enterprise middleware architecture — with a focus on scalability, security, and compliance.

Business Value

Eliminates data silos and manual reconciliation, reducing operational overhead while creating the real-time data flows required for intelligent decision-making and AI-readiness.

Example Outcomes

  • HL7 FHIR integration connecting 8 clinical systems, enabling real-time patient data access
  • Enterprise integration layer reducing manual data reconciliation by 90%
  • API ecosystem design enabling third-party integration for a financial services platform
  • Cross-entity data governance framework for a government ministry managing 15 agencies
06

Custom Digital Solutions

When off-the-shelf solutions cannot address the specific complexity of your organization, Hanova designs and builds purpose-built digital platforms — combining deep operational understanding with technical engineering to create solutions that fit your workflows, not the other way around.

Our custom development practice covers intelligent automation, clinical and operational management systems, executive reporting platforms, and specialized tools that address gaps no vendor product can fill.

Business Value

Provides organizations with proprietary digital assets that create sustainable competitive advantage — tools that are designed around your specific operational logic and cannot be replicated by competitors using standard vendor products.

Example Outcomes

  • Custom KPI governance platform deployed across a multi-entity hospital group
  • Intelligent operations dashboard integrating 5 source systems for real-time facility management
  • Regulatory compliance tracking system for a government health authority
  • Revenue intelligence platform providing predictive cash flow forecasting for a hospital network
